This page lists the block categories available in Trailmakers and summarises what each type does. Individual blocks have unique shapes, weights and functions.

Frame Blocks

Frame blocks are the structural building pieces for your vehicles. They make up the internal and external framework and come in different sizes and shapes【300239356537492†L160-L165】.

  • Blocks: basic 1x2, 1x3, 2x2 and larger blocks with studs on all sides.
  • Grid Blocks: metal poles for creating grid structures【923499217687880†L139-L151】.
  • Connectors: flat bars with limited studs used to connect parts without over‑connecting【923499217687880†L153-L157】.
  • Shields: armor panels to protect your build【923499217687880†L160-L162】.

Propulsion Blocks

These blocks provide power or thrust for your vehicles, including engines, hover pads, thrusters and propellers【300239356537492†L167-L185】.

  • Engine Blocks: engines like the Bulldawg or Dragon engine provide drive power for land vehicles. They vary in weight and acceleration【300239356537492†L169-L175】.
  • Hover Blocks: hover pads allow vehicles to float above the ground【300239356537492†L176-L178】.
  • Thruster Blocks: thrusters supply forward thrust for land and air vehicles【300239356537492†L179-L183】.
  • Propeller Blocks: propellers and heli engines provide lift and thrust in air or underwater【300239356537492†L183-L185】.

Wheels

Wheels help your vehicles move on the ground and come in sizes from small go‑kart wheels to large monster truck wheels【300239356537492†L194-L201】. Choose wheels based on the terrain and vehicle type.

Mechanical Blocks

Mechanical blocks provide movement for your vehicle. They include hinges, suspension, servos and other parts used to steer, absorb shocks or rotate components【229854249382519†L154-L156】.

  • Hinges: steering hinges and trailer hinges enable rotation【229854249382519†L159-L179】.
  • Suspension: straight suspension and suspension springs absorb shock for smoother driving【229854249382519†L182-L194】.
  • Servos: rotating and spinning servos rotate parts slowly or quickly【229854249382519†L198-L212】.
  • Pistons & Detachable Blocks: pistons extend and retract; detachable blocks allow pieces to be released.

Aerodynamic Blocks

Aerodynamic blocks help air flow smoothly around your build, improving speed. Wedges, rounded and corner blocks can be used to shape your vehicle【300239356537492†L216-L225】.

Lights

Lights illuminate your builds and add dramatic effects. There are several headlight and twilight blocks【300239356537492†L229-L236】.

Lift & Drag Blocks

Lift and drag blocks such as wings and tailfins generate lift or drag for aircraft【300239356537492†L239-L253】.

Tube Blocks

Tube blocks are modular tubes, including a unique 1x1x1 block with six connection points【300239356537492†L260-L265】.

Balance Blocks

Balance blocks adjust the weight or buoyancy of your build. Weight blocks make vehicles heavier and more stable; buoyancy blocks let builds float or become submarines【300239356537492†L267-L277】.

Gadgets

Gadgets are special equipment blocks with various uses, from adding functionality to creating complex contraptions【300239356537492†L279-L283】.

Weapons

Weapon blocks introduce cannons and other armaments for combat【300239356537492†L288-L291】.
