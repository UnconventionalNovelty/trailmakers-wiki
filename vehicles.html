Planes & Jets

Planes and jets rely on lift from wings and tailfins and thrust from engines or thrusters. To build a stable aircraft, ensure the centre of lift sits just behind the centre of mass, align thrust with the centre of mass and use control surfaces for pitch, yaw and roll.

Steps

  1. Start with a light frame for the fuselage using frame blocks.
  2. Add wings and tailfins from the lift and drag category to generate lift.
  3. Mount engines or thrusters aligned with the fuselage to provide forward thrust.
  4. Use control surfaces (e.g. hinges or servos connected to small fins) for pitch and roll control.
  5. Adjust weight distribution and wing positions until the plane is stable.

Cars

Cars are ground vehicles that rely on wheels for movement. Build a sturdy chassis, add an engine and ensure proper steering and suspension.

Steps

  1. Create a rectangular frame to serve as the chassis.
  2. Attach wheels; use steering hinges on the front wheels for turning.
  3. Place an engine block or thrusters to drive the rear wheels or all wheels as needed.
  4. Add suspension blocks for smoother handling on rough terrain.
  5. Test and adjust the weight distribution to keep the car stable and prevent flipping.

Mechs

Mechs are walking machines that require mechanical blocks like hinges, servos and pistons to simulate legs. Balance and control are critical.

Steps

  1. Design a sturdy torso using frame blocks.
  2. Use pairs of hinges and servos to create articulated legs.
  3. Add pistons or servos for knee and ankle movement.
  4. Balance the weight so the mech can stand without tipping over.
  5. Use logic blocks to coordinate leg movement for walking.

Weapon Vehicles

Weapon vehicles are equipped with cannons, rockets or other weapon blocks. They require stability and proper aiming mechanisms.

Steps

  1. Construct a stable platform or vehicle chassis using frame and balance blocks.
  2. Mount weapon blocks securely, ensuring they have a clear line of fire.
  3. Use mechanical blocks like hinges or servos to allow the weapons to aim.
  4. Install engines or thrusters to move the vehicle into position.
  5. Add logic blocks to control firing and aiming if necessary.
